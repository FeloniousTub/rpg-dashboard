Rules & assumptions used by this simulator
  • Player weapon damage: (STR + ATK) × (weapon DMG + class weapon bonus) + flat bonuses (special ability, beast weakness).
  • Ranged (Bow): (STR + ATK) × arrow DMG. Consumes 1 arrow per shot. Can hit any lane.
  • Spell damage: ATK × spell power + terrain adjustment (±3) + beast weakness. Can hit any lane. Spell cooldown applies after cast.
  • Beast damage: STR + ATK (confirmed). Deterministic — no dice rolls.
  • Defence: flat reduction. Player DEF = class DEF + equipped armour DEF − active debuffs. Minimum damage 0.
  • Lineup: melee hits its own lane; if the opposing lane is empty, melee may pick any target (unmatched rule). Beasts hit the player in their lane, else the lowest occupied lane. Invisible players are skipped.
  • Turn order: players act first (lane order), then beasts, then bleed effects, then end-of-turn regen. Cooldowns tick down at end of round.
  • Boss: enemies skip their attack in round 1 (your free turn to flee/act).
  • Kingkiller: flat +25 on the turn's weapon attack (confirmed). Unique Eunuch: +5 weapon & +5 ATK for 2 turns. My Precious: untargetable 2 turns. Waterproof Bandaid: not usable in combat — excluded.
  • Beast abilities modelled: Spit Blind (DEF −10, 3 turns, L4+), Piranha Bleed (15 × 3 turns, L3+), Scorpion Bleed (50% next turn / 25% after, L3+ assumed), Dragon Snow (+15 attack, L2+). Unknown L3+ "debuff" is logged but not simulated. Bunyip Fear (15%/lvl) is random in the real game — logged for manual adjudication; use "Pass (feared/stunned)" if it lands.
