Heal vs Regeneration
Regeneration (passive)
The Regen stat triggers at the end of every turn, after all actions including creature attacks.
Heal spells (active)
Heal spells activate on the caster's turn, before end-of-turn regeneration. Provides immediate HP recovery mid-round.
Damage formulas
Weapon damage
(Strength + Attack) x Weapon + bonuses
Spell damage
Attack x Spell damage + bonuses
Key mechanics
Defence & regen
Take 25 damage -> Defence 5 reduces to 20 -> End-of-turn Regen 5 reduces net to 15.
Turn order
Randomised each round. Creatures attack after all players. Each turn = one Earth day. Specials available from turn 1.
Lineup
Runs left to right. Position 1 can only hit opposing position 1 unless you have range. Larger lineup can attack unmatched opponents. Area damage ignores lineup.
Hands & weapons
You have 2 hands. Most weapons use 1 hand - can pair with a shield or spell. Can hold 2 spells. Only attack with 1 hand per turn. Most items have 3 levels.
Boss creatures
You get 1 full turn to flee before a boss attacks. Encountering one early? Running is wise.
Creature spawning
Mob spawn: 35% main terrain | 50% OP. No new mobs spawn mid-battle, but wanderers from unfinished battles can arrive.
Player death
Dead players still plan with team. Final blow earns 1 item of choice + 250 XP x enemy level. All other items vanish. Coins & quest items cannot be looted.
Team leader
Communicates moves to admin. Provides a stat boost nearby. On death: no new leader - dead leader still manages admin comms.
